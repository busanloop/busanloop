This is where I begin to archive the loop — the ongoing cycle of meeting people, joining conversations, and discovering what connects us all back to Busan.

I've spent years at the intersection of startups and community in this city. The people I've met, the events I've attended, the ideas that emerged from late-night conversations — they deserve a place to live beyond memory.

This blog is that place. No comments, no metrics, no algorithms. Just writing.

If you're here, you probably know me already. Welcome to the loop.
